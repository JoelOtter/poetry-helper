Joel's Super Cool Sestina Helper

Sestinas are really cool, but working out the permutations of the six words can be tiring. This tool will give randomly-generated stanza endings, obeying the rules, for a sestina using your chosen six words - hopefully you'll be able to write around them.

Just whack your words in the boxes below and hit the button!

Tool designed and built by Joel Auterson. Hope it helped (or at least provided some entertainment).
